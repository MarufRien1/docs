Request Data Deletion

If you’d like to delete some or all of your personal data without removing your Bangla Dictionary Plus account, follow these instructions. This page is provided by Bangla Dictionary Plus (Developer: Maruf R.).

Available Data Deletion Options

  • Search History: Remove all past lookup records.
  • Bookmarks & Favorites: Delete saved words and custom folders.
  • Submitted Content: Erase your example sentences and votes.
  • Personal Settings: Reset UI preferences (theme, font size, notifications).

How to Request

  1. Open the Bangla Dictionary Plus app.
  2. Navigate to Settings > Privacy > Manage Data.
  3. Select the categories you wish to delete.
  4. Tap “Delete Selected Data” and confirm.

What Stays & What’s Removed

Upon confirmation:

  • Removed: All selected items, as outlined above.
  • Retained: Your account credentials (email, username) to keep you able to log in.

Retention Notice

Deleted data is removed from active servers immediately. Backups containing deleted data may persist for up to 90 days before full expiration.
