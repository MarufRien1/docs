Request Account Deletion

If you no longer wish to use Bangla Dictionary Plus and want your entire account removed, follow the instructions below. This page is provided by Bangla Dictionary Plus (Developer: Maruf R.).

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Bangla Dictionary Plus app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings > Privacy > Delete My Account.
  3. Confirm your decision by tapping “Delete Account”.
  4. You will receive an email at your registered address asking you to confirm.
  5. Click the confirmation link in that email to complete the deletion process.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your user profile (username, email address).
  • All bookmarks, history, and personalized settings.
  • Submitted example sentences and contributions.

Retention Notice

After you confirm deletion:

  • Data will be purged from active servers immediately.
  • Backups may persist for up to 90 days for disaster recovery, after which they are permanently deleted.

If you encounter any issues, please contact us at privacy@bangladictionaryplus.com.
